Book Donations

A simple donation, can bring many smiles around.

How to donate

Wrap the book in bubble wap

Sandwich the book with two pieces of cardbox

Put the product into a similar size box

Ship it to us

Questions

Where do my books go?

Every book you drop off will be put to good use. Books are sold online to help raise funds for awesome non-profit literacy organizations changing the world through teaching kids, supporting families, building schools and filling libraries. In fact, a percentage of the net sale price of every book goes to these great causes. Any books we can’t sell are either donated directly to one of our non-profit partners or recycled.

Thanks for your interest in donating your used books to The Story Keeper. You can drop off books any day during regular business hours (Monday-Saturday, 10am-6pm; Sunday 10am-4pm). Each customer/household/family is limited to two boxes at a time.


What do we provide in return?

We will provide a 50% allowance of the shipping fee to show our gratitude. Good deeds of yours will somehow reward you in the future. Spread kindness as much as you can!


Our Intrest

We are interested in: Used books in very good condition and in paperback or Hardcover books; textbooks; magazines, periodicals, digests; books on tape or CD; “collector’s editions”; rare or antiquarian books.


Not intrested in

We are not interested in: Reader’s Digest or other “club” books; dated books; books that are heavily worn, creased, have been written in, are dog eared, or are falling apart.
